For the bookmark project, we started by sketching up 5 designs and then narrowing down to our two favorites. We used InDesign to edit and create these designs on a 2x8 in page that we would later print. Next, we picked our favorite designs and combined them into a group sheet with our classmate's designs. We then began the offset litho printing process. We needed to start by making a printing plate with our designs on it by sending our file to the Presstek platemaker. The plate got a special type of hole punch that way it would line up with the Ryobi press. We printed our sheets and let them dry overnight, the following class we trimmed them to the perfect size. We then wrapped them up and sent them off to Eshleman Elementary. On the right are my two favorite designs I made, the final design I chose was the fish bookmark!
